Welcome to my website!

I am pursuing Master's degree in Robotics and Autonomous Systems at Univeristy of Lübeck, Germany. While my coursework has exposed me to different domains like Kinematics, Control Theory and Machine Learning, I discovered my interests towards Machine Learning and Computer Vision. Inspite of the fact that my previous works mostly revolve around training machine learning models, I greatly enjoy going back to the basic mathematics very often to get my fundamentals right.

AI in Robotics have already proven to be successful in warehouses and in other mundane tasks. But we are still years away from the future where robots can be as good as humans (or even better :p). As a graduate student, I am being equipped for landing this future and would love to help these machines in the backend to 'see', 'understand' and 'learn' from the world around them.
